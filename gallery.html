Lifta, Palestine  ·  لِفْتا

Lifta was one of the largest and wealthiest Palestinian villages in the Jerusalem region. Its 2,958 inhabitants were expelled in January and February 1948, in the opening weeks of the Nakba. These photographs document what remains.
